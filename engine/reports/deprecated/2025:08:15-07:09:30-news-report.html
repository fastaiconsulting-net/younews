What Happened - On August 14, 2025, the European Commission announced a reduction in its 2025 Eurozone growth forecast to 0.9%, down from the previous estimate of 1.3%. Additionally, Germany, the bloc's largest economy, is now expected to experience zero growth in 2025, a significant downgrade from the earlier projection of 0.7%.
