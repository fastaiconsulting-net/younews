EU and US finalize trade deal imposing a 15% tariff on most European exports, aiming to prevent a broader trade conflict. The agreement includes EU commitments to invest $600 billion in the US and purchase $750 billion in American energy products, while key sectors like cars and pharmaceuticals face tariffs, with some exemptions.
