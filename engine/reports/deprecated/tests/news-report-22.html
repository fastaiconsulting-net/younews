  • Cape Verde Declares State of Emergency Amid Deadly Flooding: Cape Verde has declared a state of emergency on the islands of São Vicente and Santo Antão following deadly floods that killed at least nine people and displaced 1,500 residents. The flash floods were triggered by Tropical Storm Erin, leading to 193mm of rain in just five hours, far exceeding São Vicente’s annual average. Rescue teams are actively searching for missing individuals, while infrastructure has been severely damaged. (businesstechafrica.co.za)

Secretary of State Marco Rubio stated that the officials were complicit with the Cuban regime's labor export scheme in the Mais Medicos program. (timesofindia.indiatimes.com)

The declaration makes $750,000 available to address the surge in criminal activity and associated issues. (timesofindia.indiatimes.com)
